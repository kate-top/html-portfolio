Movies I recently liked

Luxembourg Luxembourg

This is a new Ukrainian movie by director Antonio Lukich starring one of my favorite modern music bands.

Saltburn

This is an action-packed drama with a romantic undertone that portrays a power imbalance of the modern world and an individual's ambition to rise to the top of the social pyramid, no matter the cost.

Pamfir

This movie portrays a celebration of a traditional Ukrainian holiday that unfolds in the complex socio-economic setting of the Bukovyna region. Its visuals are captivating and costume design combined with camera work creates an unforgettable atmosphere, reminiscent of Tini Zabutykh Predkiv, a Ukrainian classic.
